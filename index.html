Escape to Marawara

Use the crystals to escape certain death in the tombs of Bar Sholtan.

The Rules of the Game

The admonition is clear:

Heed these words if you wish to escape the caverns of Bar Sholtan, realm of the dead. The unicorn crystals in front of you are the key to your escape - you will only know their numerical value by touching them. You must touch the appropriate crystals to bring their sum to the magical number emblazoned in the air in front of you. Equal the number and the doors to Marawara, land of the living, shall open wide before you. Surpass the number and you are doomed forever to wander the halls of the dead. Choose wisely traveler...
